FAQ

Questions, answered straight.

If something here is unclear, we'd rather say so than oversell. Reach out and we'll add it to this list.

Does MLB Edge give picks or guaranteed winners?

No. MLB Edge is an analytics workspace. We surface public data and model outputs so you can do your own research. Nothing in the product is a recommendation, and no outcome is guaranteed.

Where does the data come from?

Publicly available sources, including MLB's Statcast feeds and standard public box-score data. We do not use proprietary, insider, or non-public information.

Is MLB Edge affiliated with MLB or a sportsbook?

No. MLB Edge is independent and has no affiliation, partnership, or revenue-share arrangement with Major League Baseball, any team, or any sportsbook.

What platforms do you support?

The web app works on modern desktop and mobile browsers through Streamlit, and the app includes iPhone and Android install instructions for home-screen access.

How accurate are the models?

We publish methodology and back-test summaries in the app changelog. Baseball is famously noisy at the single-game level — treat any model output as one input among many.

How much does MLB Edge cost?

MLB Edge is free to use and does not currently have paid plans, subscriptions, or a credit-card requirement.
